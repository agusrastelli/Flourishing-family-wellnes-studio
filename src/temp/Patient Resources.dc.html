What to expect

Your first visit, and the road after it.

This isn’t a quick-fix, one-and-done kind of place. Real change is a relationship — here’s how it unfolds.

01

A real conversation

We start by listening — your history, your goals, the things others brushed off. One-on-one with a doctor, always.

02

A thorough assessment

We find what’s actually going on and build a plan around you — measurable and explained, never generic.

03

Care over time

Progress reviews and adjustments as your body changes, with the same doctor who started with you.

Set your expectations honestly: this is a 6–12 month journey.

Meaningful, lasting change takes time. We’d rather tell you that up front than oversell a single visit. If you’re looking for a one-time crack-and-go, we’re probably not your place — and we’ll say so.
